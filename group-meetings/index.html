Group meetings¶

  • What’s the point of a group meeting?

    • Learning how to present?

    • Reading journal articles?

    • Learning how to research?

  • Most of my past research group meetings have been rather uneducational - the focus has been presenting something that looks good (in other words, presentation practice), rather than a time to focus on learning how to get those results better.

  • Often there can be a pressure to look good and hide the parts that didn’t work well (= the most education parts).

  • Can group meetings become a time to learn from each other, not a chore to attend and something that induces stress to prepare for?
